PRIVACY POLICY
AlarmApp
Last updated: March 14, 2026

AlarmApp is a local desktop application. This policy describes what data it
stores and what it does not do.

DATA STORED LOCALLY
  - Timer configurations (name, duration, alarm duration) are saved to:
      %LOCALAPPDATA%\AlarmApp\timers.json
  - This file is stored only on your device and is never transmitted anywhere.

DATA NOT COLLECTED
  - AlarmApp does not collect, transmit, or share any personal data.
  - AlarmApp does not connect to the internet.
  - AlarmApp does not use analytics, telemetry, or crash reporting.
  - AlarmApp does not use cookies or tracking of any kind.

THIRD-PARTY SERVICES
  - None. AlarmApp has no third-party integrations.

CHANGES TO THIS POLICY
  If this policy changes in a future version, the updated date above will
  reflect that change.

CONTACT
  If you have questions, open an issue in the project repository
  at https://github.com/neo99/alarm2/issues
